# Executive Summary

## Overview

The Travel Agency Services Platform is a centralized web-based application designed to connect travelers, travel agencies, and drivers through a streamlined booking and trip management system.

The platform digitizes traditional transportation booking workflows that are commonly managed through phone calls and messaging applications. Users can search destinations, discover available agencies and drivers, create bookings, and track booking statuses throughout the trip lifecycle.

Drivers, agencies, and administrators can manage travel availability, operational workflows, and booking activities through dedicated dashboards.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of the platform is to modernize regional travel booking operations by providing:

* Centralized booking management
* Destination-based service discovery
* Driver availability management
* Booking lifecycle tracking
* Administrative operational oversight
